    • Privacy

    We at Calendar Bro collect information about you in the following ways:

    1. Information about you when you register an event.
    2. Information about you when you order a ticket.
    3. Information about how you’ve visited us (cookies).

    Number 1

    When you register an event with us, we save your information on our secure servers. We always use SSL for all our pages. When you give us this information you agree to our terms and conditions and allow us to store your details.

    Number 2

    When you order a ticket from us we collect data from you for the organiser of the event so that they can identify you. We always use SSL for all our pages. When you give us this information you agree to our terms and conditions and allow us to store your details.

    Number 3 (cookies)

    We use the third party service called Google Analytics to analyse you as an entity (i.e. what browser you’re using, which country you’re from etc). This helps us to improve our service as best we can. If you do not want us to collect this information about you then due to technical difficulties we would advise you not to use our service.

    Other things to note

    We care about your data (and ours) so we will never share your details (organiser or attendee) with any third party company or organisation.
