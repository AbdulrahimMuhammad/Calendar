    • About

    We at Calendar Bro know it can get cumbersome to offer tickets online for small events especially if it’s at the very last minute so we created this service for those who need a quick and easy way to do exactly that.

    We’ve kept everything to a minimum, from the settings to the design, to give you and your attendees an awesome experience. Offer your tickets, get the orders in, export the information, delete everything and start over or create a new event with a new URL.

    You can sell tickets via Paypal or give them away for free, it’s up to you. Once you’re setup direct potential attendees to your custom event page and see the orders rolling in. Get started today by registering your event and we’re always here to help so contact us if you need anything.
