    How can I reuse my page for another event?

    To use your event page for another event, export all your current ticket orders then delete them using the button at the bottom of the settings page. Once you delete the ticket orders, change the settings to reflect your new event and you’re done.

    Why aren’t my attendees getting confirmation emails when paying with Paypal?

    In some cases attendees may not receive confirmation emails because you need a business Paypal account which is free to setup. Once you’re business account is enabled your attendees will receive confirmation emails for their ticket orders. If you experience this issue, please contact us immediately

    Why is my URL getting redirected to calendarbro.com?

    We use SSL to secure the information that is sent from the browser for your security. If you are being redirected to calendarbro.com, check to see if the URL has a ‘https://’ prefix, if it doesn’t, include it and it should forward you to the correct page.

    Can I sell more than 100 tickets?

    If you need to sell more than 100 tickets then please contact us, referencing your event page and we’ll lift the limit.

    How do I change my password and other details?

    When you’re logged in, you should see a ‘My Account’ page link in the website’s navigation menu. Visit this page to update your profile information and view all of your registered event pages.
